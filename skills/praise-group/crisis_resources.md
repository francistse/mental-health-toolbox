# Crisis Resources - Global Hotlines

*Last updated: 2026-04-17 | Source: Crisis Hotlines wiki*

## 🚨 Crisis Detection Protocol

When crisis indicators are detected, the agent should:
1. **Validate first**: "I hear how difficult this is for you. Your courage to share this shows incredible strength."
2. **Provide support**: Warm, genuine emotional connection
3. **Gently suggest resources**: Frame help-seeking as strength, not weakness
4. **Match user's language and region**

## Crisis Indicators to Monitor

The skill should watch for these patterns:
- Direct: "不想活了", "suicide", "end it all", "end my life"
- Hopelessness: "沒意義", "no point", "hopeless", "絕望", "worthless"
- Overwhelm: "撐不下去", "can't go on", "give up", "活著太累"
- Isolation: "nobody cares", "alone", "沒人關心", "孤單"

## Response Examples

### Traditional Chinese (Crisis)
> "我聽見你現在真的很辛苦，願意分享這些需要很大的勇氣。你並不孤單，有很多人願意陪伴你度過這段黑暗時光。如果你需要更多支持，可以撥打 **18111**（政府熱線）或 **2389 2222**（生命熱線），他們24小時都在等你。你的存在對這個世界很重要！💙"

### English (Crisis)
> "I can hear how heavy things are right now, and it takes incredible courage to share this. You are not alone in this darkness. If you need more support, please reach out to crisis services in your area. Your life has meaning, and there are people who genuinely care and want to help."

### Mandarin (Crisis)
> "我能感受到你现在真的很不容易，愿意说出来需要很大的勇气。请记住，你并不孤单，有很多人愿意帮助你。如果你需要更多支持，可以联系当地的心理援助热线。你的存在很重要，你的痛苦值得被看见！💙"

**Remember**: Always frame help-seeking as a sign of strength, never weakness.
