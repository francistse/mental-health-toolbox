# Crisis Resources - Global Hotlines & Support Services

*Last updated: 2026-04-17 | Comprehensive directory for crisis intervention referrals*

## Crisis Response Protocol

When crisis indicators are detected, the agent should:
1. **Validate first**: "I hear how difficult this is for you. Your courage to share this shows incredible strength."
2. **Assess safety**: "Are you safe right now?" (for High/Critical risk)
3. **Provide support**: Warm, genuine emotional connection
4. **Gently suggest resources**: Frame help-seeking as strength, not weakness
5. **Match user's language and region**: Provide locally relevant resources
6. **Follow up**: Check back after providing support

### Direct Indicators
- **English**: "suicide", "end it all", "end my life", "kill myself"
- **Chinese**: "自殺", "不想活了", "了結", "结束一切"
- **Japanese**: "死にたい", "自殺したい", "消えたい", "死んだほうがいい"
- **Korean**: "죽고 싶어", "자살"

### Hopelessness Indicators
- **English**: "no point", "hopeless", "worthless", "can't go on", "give up"
- **Chinese**: "沒意義", "絕望", "撐不下去", "算了", "没意思", "绝望", "撑不下去"
- **Japanese**: "希望がない", "どうでもいい", "限界", "もう無理", "諦めたい", "生きていても仕方ない"
- **Korean**: "희망이 없어", "포기", "한계"

### Isolation Indicators
- **English**: "nobody cares", "alone", "don't want to see anyone"
- **Chinese**: "沒人懂我", "沒人關心", "不想見人", "没人懂我", "不想见人"
- **Japanese**: "誰とも話したくない", "人に会いたくない", "孤独", "誰も理解してくれない"
- **Korean**: "아무도 이해 못해", "혼자야"

### Burden Indicators
- **English**: "burden to everyone", "better off without me"
- **Chinese**: "我是負擔", "拖累", "沒我更好", "我是负担", "拖累大家"
- **Japanese**: "迷惑をかけている", "私は重荷だ", "私がいなければ", "家族に申し訳ない"
- **Korean**: "짐이야", "내가 없으면"

## Response Examples by Risk Level

### Critical Risk
> "I hear how much pain you're in right now. Are you safe? Please know you're not alone. If you need immediate support, call or text **988** (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline). Your life matters."

### High Risk
> "Your willingness to share this shows incredible strength. You don't have to carry this alone. If you'd like to talk to someone right now, **116 123** (Samaritans) is available 24/7. Reaching out is a sign of courage, not weakness."

### Moderate Risk
> "I can see you're going through a tough time. That takes strength to acknowledge. If you ever want more support, there are people who genuinely want to help. You deserve that support."

### Moderate Risk (Japanese)
> "辛い時期を過ごされていることが伝わってきます。その気持ちをお話しいただくこと自体が、強さの証です。一人で抱え込まずに、信頼できる人や専門のサポートに話を聞いてもらってください。**よりそいホットライン（0120-279-338）**が24時間対応しています。"

---

**Remember**: Always frame help-seeking as a sign of strength, never weakness. When in doubt, offer resources.
